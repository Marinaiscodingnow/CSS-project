Breakfast

For breakfast I normally have and omelet with cheese in a wrap or bagel. Sometimes if my dad has enough time he makes waffle, I like waffles with confectionary sugar. We make pancakes on weekends where we have a special tool that alows you to make pancake art. So we like to make basic animals, funny shapes, and holiday themed ones. On really special occasions we make home fries or bacon.

Lunch

It's fun when they get upset about it and you just say whoops! \(^~^)/

Dinner
